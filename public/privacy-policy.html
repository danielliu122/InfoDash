Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

InfoDash collects information to provide personalized dashboard services. The types of information we collect include:

  • User Preferences: Settings and customization options for dashboard features
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our services and features
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, and IP address for service optimization

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our dashboard services
  • To personalize your experience and remember your preferences
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To communicate with you about service updates and changes

3. Data Storage and Cookies

InfoDash uses cookies and local storage to enhance your experience:

  • Your preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • Cookies help us remember your choices and provide personalized content
  • You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences
  • Data is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary for service functionality

4. Third-Party Services

InfoDash integrates with third-party services to provide comprehensive information:

  • Financial Data: Stock market and financial information services
  • News Services: Current events and headline providers
  • Weather Services: Weather forecasts and conditions
  • Trend Analysis: Search trends and popular topics
  • AI Services: Chat functionality and content generation

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data transmission using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure storage practices for any data we maintain
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can view and manage your stored preferences
  • Modification: You can update your preferences at any time
  • Deletion: You can clear stored data through browser settings
  • Opt-out: You can disable certain features or data collection
  • Cookie Control: You can manage cookies through browser settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser remains until you clear it
  • Server Logs: Technical logs are retained for security and debugging purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage statistics may be retained for service improvement

InfoDash is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

InfoDash may process and store your information in countries other than your own. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
